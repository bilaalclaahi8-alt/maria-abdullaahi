Empowering Somali Women in Marine & Environmental Science

Building skills, sharing knowledge, and supporting coastal communities.

We are a Somali-led initiative dedicated to increasing participation of women in marine science and environmental stewardship. Join our programs, follow our research, or support our work so we can protect coastal ecosystems and empower the next generation of Somali scientists.
